The Process

We've been where you are right now.

 
The loss of control of your life and the loss of control in yourself. You know you're in trouble – you feel it every day, people you love are being hurt because of it – yet you lie to yourself.  "It's not that bad, I'll be more aware next time." The trouble is you won't. You don't know when or how to stop... but you can't stop!

The Process consists of just four phases and has been achieving amazing results with the people we help. We firmly believe that anyone who suffers from our affliction can get sober and stay sober forever if they have the capacity, to be honest and take responsibility for their actions, behaviours and their life.

Try it now

This is an exciting time and we hope that if you have been struggling and you know in your heart that you are an alcoholic and are willing to be rigorously honest and go to any length for your sobriety then we can help you and will support you on your new and exciting journey.
