Hi, I'm Paul Heffernan and I'm a recovered alcoholic with 30 years sobriety.
Let me help you stop drinking for good.

I've been where you are right now. The loss of control of your life and the loss of control in yourself. You know you're in trouble - you feel it every day, people you love are being hurt because of it - yet you lie to yourself. "It's not that bad, I'll be more aware next time."

Trouble is you won't. You don't know how to stop... you can't stop! And you know it!

Let me take you through my program called 'The Process'. Consisting of 3 steps over 5 days. If you commit 100% you need never drink again.
