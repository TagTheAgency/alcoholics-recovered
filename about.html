ABOUT ME

Paul Heffernan a recovered alcoholic with over 30 years sobriety founded the Recovered Group. Paul has successfully help people stop drinking. Fuelled by a passion to help more escape the bonds of Alcoholism Paul developed the Recovered Group with a focus on using Paul’s passion for technology and love for people, the Recovered Group has built systems to not only help people stop drinking but to support and maintain an alcohol free lifestyle.

ABOUT ME

Hi, I'm Paul Heffernan and I'm a recovered alcoholic with 30 years sobriety

Paul — "During my own journey, I attended countless meetings, treatment centres, counsellors and clinicians in the first 18 years. I learnt and witnessed so much of how destructive alcoholism can be and while I'm so grateful to have remained sober I wanted to find a more effective way of helping people like me.

For the next 12 years, I analysed and worked with 100's of alcoholics many of whom were regular meeting attendees, some of them would eventually drink again. Why? What was the mechanism that made it so hard for them to stay sober? There had to be a very deep-seated reason. I had to find it.

When I found this information, it made me realise that I needed to create an environment where alcoholics who wanted to get well, could get well, stay sober and live a happy and contented life. That is if they wanted to and were ready, to be honest and take full responsibility for their sobriety, their life and their actions. So I created the Recovered Group"
